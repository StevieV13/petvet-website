About PetVet

Built by pet owners, for pet owners.

PetVet was founded with a simple belief: responsible pet ownership starts with knowing your animal's health history. Too many South African pets miss critical vaccinations, treatments get forgotten, and vet visits go undocumented.

We built the platform we wished existed — one that makes accountability effortless, keeps records permanent, and puts owners in control of their pet's wellbeing.
